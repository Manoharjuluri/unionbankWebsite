Top Benefits of Home Loan Vs Cash Payment while Buying House



Top Benefits Of Home Loan Vs Cash Payment While Buying A House!

  1. Lower Home Loan Interest Rates

    A house loan has a substantially lower interest rate than any other sort of loan that is offered. In order to get out of financial trouble, you might be able to top up your current house loan at a cheaper interest rate than a personal loan.

  2. Tax Benefits

    A home loan has various tax benefits. If you have a home loan, you can exclude interest and principal payments from your taxable income. Owners can deduct up to Rs. 2 lahks in interest on their house loan for a self-occupied/ let out property under section 24 of the Income Tax

  3. Liquidity

    It is better and more sensible to save or invest any liquid funds you have on hand. Because life is chaotic, our plans frequently become wrecked. A liquidity problem could arise down the road if you use up all of your available cash to buy a house in one go. While only paying a portion of your income in home loan EMIs, home loans allow you to maintain your liquidity.

  4. Repayment Tenure

    Only home loans permit a payback period of up to 30 years. As a result, extending the term will help you pay off your debt more quickly. Use a straightforward online tool, like the home loan EMI calculator, to determine how your EMI will alter when the tenure changes.

  5. Less Burden

    The only lump sum payment required when applying for a home loan in India is the one you make as a down payment. As a result, the impact of making a large payment is spread out across EMIs.
